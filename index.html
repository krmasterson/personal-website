Rentie.ca

Rentie is a Ruby on Rails app my team and I built over a 2 week period. The app is built using Ruby on Rails, Javascript, Html, and CSS. It allows property owners to manage their daily tasks and contact list, view their finances as a whole and also sorted by individual building, and add or delete a building or unit from their profile. The tenant side of the app allows tenants to submit maintenance requests directly to the property owner and pay their rent online using a secure payment platform.
